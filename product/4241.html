  • The Knight of Apocalypse, the End of the World -
  • From the episode "Mobile Suit Gundam Gaiden Missing Link" included in the game software "Mobile Suit Gundam Side Stories",
  • The Pale Rider was captured by the Zeon forces and has been extensively renovated, "Toris Ritter", now in the HG series with a completely new model.
  • ● Toris Ritter has been made into a three-dimensional model with a completely new model, and is equipped with a variety of gimmicks!
  • In addition to reproducing the characteristic aircraft shape, it is equipped with gimmicks such as sub-arm deployment on the shoulder and Incom injection mounted on the backpack.
  • Body shape
  • The parts that have been renovated since "Pale Rider" over the course of 10 years in the play are reproduced with completely new modeling.
  • Faithfully reproduces every detail from the head antenna to the increased thrusters on the legs and the tips of the feet.
  • Shoulder/Sub arm
  • The shoulder part with a built-in sub-arm is three-dimensional.
  • Furthermore, the arm can be equipped with a hyper beam saber.
  • backpack
  • Reproduces a large backpack equipped with a central sub-generator, 6 verniers, left and right thruster binders, and a propellant tank.
  • The six incoms installed in the thruster binder can be removed to reproduce the ejection state using lead wires.
  • ● Includes various weapons that have been strengthened through renovation!
  • Weapons appropriated from Neo Zeon MS are reproduced with new modeling.
  • hyper knuckle buster
  • A high-power shooting weapon that was adapted from "Ga-Zoum" in the movie.
  • shield
  • A shield adapted from "Bow".
  • beam saber
  • Can be stored in the forearm.
  • ● You can reproduce the appearance when HADES is activated!
  • Normal state and HADES activated state can be reproduced by selecting clear molded visor and seal.
  • Comes with two colored visors: clear green and clear red!
  • Attached weapons: Hyper Knuckle Buster / Hyper Beam Saber / Beam Saber / Incom / Shield
