  • ■The molding color is now clear, making the internal mechanisms of the Gundam frame and Ahab Reactor even more appealing!

  • ■The internal frame, cylinder, and armor are linked by a movable axis built into the upper body.
  • ■The cylinder on the shoulder reproduces the mechanical structure that follows the movement of pulling out.
  • ■The frame and cylinder of the knees move in conjunction with each other.
  • ■In the standby position, swings to the left and right and movement of the hip joint axis are reproduced.
  • ■3 types of interchangeable hand parts are included.
  • ■The grip and tip of the mace can be slid!
  • ■The barrel of the smooth cannon is equipped with a sliding/folding gimmick.
