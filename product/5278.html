  • Armed with the wings of destiny, Impulse launches!
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam SEED DESTINY MSV", Destiny Impulse equipped with Destiny Silhouette is now available in real grade.
  • In addition to the new backpack design, the main body is also reproduced in a molding color that matches the image.
  • The detailed expression and range of motion unique to RG are condensed into 1/144 scale!
  • ● Body color reproduction by molding color
  • The aircraft color of Destiny Impulse, which is based on the characteristic reddish-purple and white, is reproduced with molded colors.
  • ● Thoroughly reproduce the ‘Destiny Silhouette’ and body shape with new modeling
  • The large backpack 'Destiny Silhouette' equipped on the back has been thoroughly reproduced with new modeling.
  • In addition, the flash edge beam boomerangs equipped on both arms are also reproduced with new parts.
  • Telescopic barrel extended beam turret
  • The left and right pair of beam cannons are ``telescopic barrel extension type beam turret'', and the arms of the backpack can be expanded to recreate shooting poses.
  • As an RG original setting, it can also be removed and held with both hands.
  • Excalibur laser anti-ship sword
  • The Excalibur laser anti-ship sword can be mounted on a backpack.
  • The mounting base can be moved flexibly.
  • Wings of Light effect parts (for RG 1/144 Destiny Gundam) can be attached.
  • *Not included with this product.
  • ● Water transfer decal included!
  • Comes with an original designed water transfer decal to create the Destiny Impulse body!
