  • ■The head is divided into detailed parts to reproduce color coding.
  • ■Equipped with joint parts made of KPS material to realize powerful action scenes.
  • ■The arm can be bent naturally by moving the armored parts.
  • ■It is also possible to reproduce the single Harken form by removing the included Double Harken.
  • ■The back side of the clear parts is also processed to create a sense of precision.
  • ■In addition to the main weapons, 2 types of hand parts, left and right, and a double spacer are included.
  • ■It can be combined with the included double spacer and further combined with the pedestal to recreate the flight state.
