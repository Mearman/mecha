  • From ``Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack'', the blue Gira Doga controlled by Rezun Schneider appears in master grade!
  • Gira Doga, piloted by Ensign Rezun Schneider, one of the Neo Zeon MS unit's leading ace pilots, has been commercialized in a special aircraft color.
  • ● The impressive color of the Rezun dedicated machine, which is a symbolic ace machine in the play, is faithfully reproduced with molded color!
  • ● Adopts a movable mechanism for each joint that allows dynamic action poses to be reproduced.
  • ● The mono-eye uses a mechanism that swings from side to side, and an optional LED unit (pink) can be installed to reproduce the light emission of the mono-eye.
  • ● The power pipe is reproduced with soft material to pursue a realistic texture.
  • ● Comes with an emotion manipulator with movable fingers to enrich your expressions and hand parts with interchangeable units.
  • ● Comes with a variety of weapons to recreate numerous battle scenes.
  • Attached weapons: Beam machine gun (2 types) / Sturm Faust / Beam sword ax (3 types of beam effects) / Shield
