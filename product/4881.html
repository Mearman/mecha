  • --- Now available in Real Grade!
  • Gundam Astraea Type-F is reproduced in the RG series, which pursues unparalleled detail and "realism" in 1/144!
  • From the official side story "Mobile Suit Gundam 00 F", we have commercialized the Gundam Astrea Type-F, which has a distinctive red color.
  • ● Reproduce the characteristic body shape with new modeling!!
  • The head, collar, neck, chest, shoulders, abdomen, front armor, rear armor, and other parts have different shapes from Gundam Exia.
  • The sides of the arms and knees are reproduced with new modeling.
  • ● Selectable kit that allows you to reproduce Type F and Type F2 by selecting parts!!
  • The face can be reproduced with or without a mask for both types.
  • ● Includes numerous weapons designed according to RG standards!!
  • Newly included GN Launcher, Proto GN Sword, GN Beam Rifle, and GN Shield.
  • Comes with joint parts that allow you to pose with the Proto GN Sword attached to your arm and the GN Beam Rifle.
  • ● New realistic decals for Gundam Astrea Type-F are included.
  • ● Comes with a variety of weapons that expand the range of play.
  • ● Can also be equipped with each weapon from the HG series kit.
  • Attached weapons: GN launcher, proto GN sword, GN beam rifle, GN beam saber, GN beam dagger, GN shield
