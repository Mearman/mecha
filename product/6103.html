  • "Libel Knight" is now available from the new plastic model series "30 MINUTES FANTASY" where you can create your own fantasy job!

  • ■A new mechanism for attaching the shoulder and waist armor via the joint on the back allows for flexible movement.
  • ■Swords and shields can be mounted on the back or waist using joint parts.
