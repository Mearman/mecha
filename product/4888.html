Details

  • G-3 Gundam with magnetic coating is now available in the RG (Real Grade) series!
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam MSV", the RX-78-3 G-3 Gundam with its impressive image color,
  • Commercialized as a real grade series with unprecedented color-coded reproduction and flexible range of motion in 1/144 scale.
  • ● The armored parts are colored in the gradations unique to real grade, and are reproduced in molded colors with different tones!
  • ● Reproduce the magnetic coating exterior with molded parts!
  • Magnetic coating is applied to all 12 areas on the left and right, inside and outside of the elbow, knee, and ankle cover.
  • The shape of the exterior parts is reproduced with special parts.
  • Exterior parts of the same regular shape are also included.
  • ● Water transfer decal included!
  • Contains two types of markings: Gunpla original version and setting illustration version.
  • By selecting the markings, the setting illustration version can also be reproduced.
  • ● Reproduces the aircraft with different color coding from RX-78-2 Gundam with molded colors.
  • Attached weapons: Beam rifle / Hyper Bazooka / Beam saber / Shield
