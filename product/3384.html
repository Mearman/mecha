  • Introducing easy-to-use nippers with attention to detail that allow you to assemble BANDAI SPIRITS plastic models without stress!
  • ■The blade is designed for plastic models in pursuit of a balance of hardness, material, and angle.
  • ■A rod-shaped spring is used for the contact, making it difficult to disconnect and providing a soft feel.
  • ■Uses high carbon steel and is designed with a good balance of sharpness and durability.
  • ■The material of the grip is soft.
  • ■Designed to fit snugly in the palm of your hand, with uneven surfaces for your thumb to get caught in, non-slip grooves, and a curve at the end of the grip.
  • ■In order to eliminate the difficulty of using the nippers due to them opening too much, the overlapping parts of the blades are deliberately brought into contact with each other to prevent them from opening.
