Details

  • From the movie "SAND LAND" to be released in the summer of 2023, the 1/35 scale Royal Army Tank Corps No. 104 (Hitomaruyon) is commercialized as a plastic model!
  • ■With precise design unique to plastic models, such as armor welding marks, the body is approximately 110mm in height and is packed with details reminiscent of a scale model.
  • ■The main gun is movable.
  • ■The hatches installed on the left, right, and front sides of the vehicle can be opened and closed.
  • ■Two types of antenna shaft parts are included: metal and plastic resin.
  • ■Same scale character figures ``Beelzebub'', ``Thief'', and ``Rao'' in fixed poses that can be installed by opening the hatch are included.
  • ■Represents the characteristics of the tank, such as the hidden wheels and the shape of the inside of the tracks.
  • ■A display base that depicts the track tracks, rising dust, and a plate with the title logo of the work are also included.
