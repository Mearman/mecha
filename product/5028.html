  • From "Mobile Fighter G Gundam", "God Gundam" is now available in RG!
  • ■Pursuing movements that seem to trace the human body unique to Mobile Fighter.
  • ■Advanced MS joints introduced in the shoulders and wrists allow you to recreate the characteristic "arms crossed" pose.
  • ■The body has a mechanism that allows it to move forward and backward and left and right.
  • ■The neck has a 3-axis movable structure.
  • ■The expansion and contraction of muscles that occur in the human body when bending the knees is expressed by indenting a part of the back thigh parts.
  • ■A new mechanism has been adopted for the movement of the toes, making it possible to keep the feet in contact with the ground while doing almost horizontal spreads.
  • ■Multi-stage movement from the chest to the waist, and by introducing a sliding mechanism at the hip joint, it is possible to kick the legs while flipping up the front waist armor.
  • ■The movable shaft at the base of the fin allows the fin to flow backwards.
  • ■The base of the nail and the thumb cover are equipped with movable shafts to reproduce God's finger.
