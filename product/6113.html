  • ■The latest designed internal frame is inspired by a semi-monocoque structure, allowing you to relive the construction of a mobile suit.

  • ■The legs are a reconsideration of the "hand-to-hand combat MS Gundam" and use composite frame joints to draw an outline similar to that of a human when it moves.
  • ■Details are engraved on parts that are not visible from the outside, such as joints and the back of parts.
  • ■The intricately colored head is thoroughly reproduced by color-coding by molding color.
  • ■Panel lines that reflect the support from the mechanism are divided into functional blocks.
  • ■The exterior design is a fusion of the latest interpretation and a return to origins, not only by adding small details, but also by taking into account the overall shape of each part, such as the edges and curved surfaces.
