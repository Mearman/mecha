  • Hyakushiki Kai, first Gunpla kit!
  • From "Mobile Suit Z Gundam" MSV, the Hyaku-shiki Kai, a derivative of the Hyaku-shiki with excellent attack power and expandability, is commercialized in the Master Grade series.
  • Each characteristic part of the body is reproduced with new modeling, and the body color is expressed with shiny gold plating!
  • Hyaku-shiki can be reproduced by selecting parts!
  • ● Thorough reproduction of each characteristic part throughout the body!
  • Hyakushiki Kai's characteristic head, shoulder armor, arms, front armor, rear armor, legs, toes, backpack, etc.
  • Reproduced with new modeling with MG compliant details.
  • Just by assembling it, you can create a shiny exterior.
  • ・Head
  • The characteristic head, which is different from the mass-produced Hyaku-shiki Kai with its antenna and protruding shape at the rear, is reproduced in rich detail.
  • ・Backpack
  • A newly designed large backpack equipped with a drum-shaped deflection thruster and high-mobility vernier stabilizer.
  • Reproduced with new modeling with mechanical details.
  • ・Leg armor/feet
  • The legs have a unique silhouette similar to those of Delta Gundam, Delta Plus, and Gundam Delta Chi.
  • The thruster shapes on the sides of both legs are reproduced in different colors using molded parts, faithfully reproducing the shape of the long toes.
  • ● You can reproduce Hyakushiki by selecting parts!
  • A set of parts that can reproduce the 100 types is included.
  • You can enjoy the gloss-plated Hyaku-shiki, which has a significantly different impression from the MG Hyaku-shiki Ver. 2.0.
  • ● Shiny gold plating!
  • The impressive exterior of Hyaku-shiki and Hyaku-shiki Kai is reproduced with shiny gold plating.
  • Just by assembling it, you can create a shiny exterior.
  • Adopts an under gate to achieve a beautiful finish with no noticeable gate marks.
  • ● Original markings corresponding to Hyaku-shiki Kai and Hyaku-shiki are included as water transfer decals!
  • ● Comes with a variety of weapons that expand the range of poses.
  • ● Both Hyaku-shiki Kai and Hyaku-shiki are compatible with "Mega Bazooka Launcher" (sold separately) and "Valute Pack" (sold separately).
