Details

  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam UC (Unicorn) episode 7", Silva Bullet, controlled by Gael Chan, appears!
  • Silva Bullet, which reproduces the characteristics of Gael Chan's special machine with new parts and molding colors, is commercialized in the HGUC series.
  • ● Reproduce the distinctive head with new modeling!
  • ● New parts are used for the arm vernier and leg hem, and the molding color perfectly reproduces the main body color!
  • ● The beam saber is molded in clear blue to recreate the image from the movie!
  • - The beam cannon is equipped with a forward deployment gimmick, allowing you to reproduce the firing mode.
  • - Two anti-ship missiles are included and can be attached to a backpack by replacing parts.
  • - Comes with a special stand and lead wire to hold the injected forearm, allowing you to recreate the forearm injection scene.
  • - The shield uses a telescopic gimmick, allowing for impressive poses.
  • - Various wrist parts are included.
  • - Jim Head can be reproduced by selecting head parts.
  • Attached weapons: beam rifle, beam saber, anti-ship missile, shield
