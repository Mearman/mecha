Details

  • The Figure-rise LABO series challenges new expressions of plastic models.
  • From the movie "Rebuild of Evangelion: Destruction", Asuka appears wearing a "test plug suit" for the Eva Unit 3 boarding test!
  • ■Body expression through shading using molding technology.
  • ■The clear orange part of the suit that is in close contact with the skin is reproduced with a special molding of about 3 mm to about 0.3 mm at the thinnest point.
  • ■Eye tracking injection (insert molding of the eyes) realizes tracking of Asuka's line of sight as if she were staring at you.
  • ■Molds are processed taking into consideration the properties and textures of various parts that overlap in a complex manner.
  • ■The prototype was realized in collaboration with "ALTER".
