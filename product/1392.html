Details

  • ■From "Star Wars: The Force Awakens", the X-Wing Fighter Resistance version that appears in the movie is reproduced on 1/72 scale.
  • ■Details have been reproduced through thorough verification of materials and condensed into 1/72 scale.
  • ■The characteristic wings open and close as they do in the play.
  • ■The lines on the side of the fuselage reproduce the color coding using molded colors.
  • ■Two types of canopy are included: one made entirely of clear material and one with only a frame.
  • ■Landing gear is included to reproduce landing and maintenance conditions.
  • ■Laser cannons can reproduce the appearance of attacking by inserting laser effects into the wing tips.
  • ■The motif of the aircraft's display base is the desert of Jakku.
  • ■Same scale seated type pilot and BB-8 parts are included.
