  • ● Color-coded reproduction of the aircraft color in the setting draft using molding colors

  • The armor on the backs of the hands and the backs of the feet is also reproduced by color coding by dividing the parts.
  • ● Reproduce the head shape before weaponization with new modeling
  • Reproduce the shape before mounting fixed weapons such as head Vulcan and weapon container.
  • ● Comes with a newly designed decal based on the setting drawing!
  • A newly designed water transfer decal that includes line marks and caution marks unique to the setting drawing is included.
  • ● Various weapons of Gunigi are included!
