  • “Gundam” attack/defense improvement plan -FSWS-
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam" MSD (Mobile Suit Discovery),
  • The Full Armor Gundam, said to have been developed by the Earth Federation Forces' FSWS, is now available as an HG kit.
  • Various weapons and armor parts are three-dimensionalized with new modeling.
  • Includes water transfer decals including new designs.
  • ● Reproduce the color pattern of each part increased armor & MSD version with new modeling
  • The increased armor of each part of the whole body is reproduced with new modeling.
  • The shape is reproduced down to the smallest detail, including the chest duct, abdominal armor, and leg sub-thrusters.
  • ● The MSD version's aircraft coloring is reproduced with molding colors, including the base RX-78.
  • The main body of RX-78 can be replaced with armor and a small shield on the left arm.
  • ● Reproduce various weapons and their gimmicks with new modeling
  • The shapes and gimmicks of various weapons such as the cannon (360mm rocket), double beam gun, and missile bay are reproduced with new modeling.
  • Cannon (360mm rocket gun)
  • The cannon on the right back can move up and down.
  • double beam gun
  • The double beam gun attached to the right arm is precisely reproduced down to the gun barrel.
  • missile bay
  • The missile bays located on both shoulders and knees of the chest are equipped with a hatch opening/closing gimmick.
  • The shape of the internal missile is also reproduced in detail.
  • ● Comes with a special water transfer decal that includes MSD-inspired line marks on the shoulders and waist, federal mark, number mark, etc.
  • ● The movable performance of HG Gundam (GUNDAM THE ORIGIN version) allows for bold action poses while wearing armor.
  • Attached weapons: Cannon (360mm rocket cannon) / Missile bay / Dual beam gun
