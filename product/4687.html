  • ------
  • “Expo 2020 Dubai” will be held in Dubai, United Arab Emirates (UAE) from October 1, 2021.
  • "Gundam" was selected as the Japan Pavilion's PR ambassador.
  • The "Gundam" designed by Kunio Okawara, Japan's first mechanical designer for animation works, has been commercialized as a PR ambassador for the Japan Pavilion at the Dubai International Expo in the HG series.
  • Designed Gundams are consolidated into 1/144 scale Gunpla using new molds.
  • ------
  • ● Reproduced with new mold
  • Reproduce the newly designed RX-78-2 Gundam with a new mold.
  • Based on the design data of the 2015 HG 1/144 RX-78-2 Gundam, which has good proportions, a range of motion, and is easy to assemble, it has been rebuilt to achieve both color reproduction and ease of assembly.
  • The impressive coloring of the arms, abdomen, front armor, waist, legs, beam saber, etc. is reproduced in molded color by dividing the parts.
  • The white lines on the arms and legs are detailed using stickers and mold processing.
  • Reproduces the vernier added to the calf area.
  • A variety of new hand parts are included to create action scenes.
  • ● Original marking sticker included
  • Includes marking stickers inspired by Japan designed by Kunio Okawara.
  • The sticker design uses "kanji".
  • ●Special recording Interview with Kunio Okawara Special leaflet included
  • We have published a special interview with Kunio Okawara regarding the "Gundam" that will be used as PR ambassador for the Japan Pavilion at the Dubai International Expo.
  • A special leaflet is included.
  • ● Delivered in limited full color packaging
  • The package design features a beautiful contrast of red and white, with motifs of the Japan Pavilion's "soft Japanese shape" and "hemp leaf pattern."
  • Delivered in special limited edition packaging with full color printing and red foil stamping.
  • Attached weapons: Beam rifle / Beam saber / Shield
