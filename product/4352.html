Details

  • "Kamen Rider Ryuki" is now available as the 11th work in the project to make all the main character Kamen Riders of the Heisei era into plastic models!
  • ■Express the silver part with extra finish.
  • ■The opening of the head slit is realized by ultra-thin molding.
  • ■Five advent cards made of PET material are included.
  • ■A handle for cards is included, allowing you to recreate the loading image.
  • ■Drag redder with a total length of approximately 630mm is included.
  • ■By using some parts of the drag redder, it is possible to reproduce the "sword vent" and "guard vent".
  • ■With movable joints and a pedestal for drag redder, it is possible to create a display based on scenes from the movie.
  • 【accessories】

  • ■Drag claw x 1
  • ■Wrist parts Decisive hand (right), card holder (right), grasping hand (left and right), weapon holder (left and right), open hand (left and right)
