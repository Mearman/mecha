  • - ν Gundam heavy armed form reproduced in RG -
  • HWS (Heavy Weapon System), which is equipped with additional armor on the RG 1/144 ν Gundam, has been commercialized.
  • ● Reproduce HWS (Heavy Weapon System) with RG-compliant high detail.
  • The heavy armor consisting of three parts: chest, waist, and legs is reproduced with new modeling.
  • The color coding of each armor panel line, mold, and internal mechanism are reproduced in high detail according to RG.
  • chest armor unit
  • Chest armor integrated with shoulder missile launcher.
  • Equipped with a deployment gimmick on the front of the armor, and the missile launcher inside is also precisely reproduced.
  • hyper mega rifle
  • Hyper Mega Rifle is reproduced with new modeling.
  • Equipped with a barrel extension gimmick, it is possible to reproduce the normal state and ultra-long range shooting state.
  • high mega shield
  • Reproduces the large high mega shield that is attached to the surface of the shield.
  • The armor on the top and sides can be expanded, and the dual mega particle cannon is equipped with a gimmick that extends the barrel forward.
  • In addition, a special attachment is included to strengthen the holding power when equipped.
  • Leg thruster unit
  • The armor and thruster unit on the side of the leg is newly modeled.
  • ● Color patterns with different impressions
  • The armor color, including the main body, uses a matte molding color that is different from "RG ν Gundam" (store item).
  • Furthermore, by adopting two types of color patterns for white, the panel lines of the armor are expressed.
  • ● Comes with a newly designed realistic decal.
  • Attached weapons: Hyper Mega Rifle / High Mega Shield / New Hyper Bazooka / Fin Funnel / Beam Rifle / Beam Saber
