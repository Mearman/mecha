  • "Improved Zeta Gundam with increased firepower"
  • Based on Z Gundam Unit 3, this aircraft has been improved with an emphasis on firepower.
  • The driving performance has also been improved with large tail stabilizers that can be deployed from side to side.
  • It is commonly known as "Gray Zeta," but its color is yellow due to the beam-resistant coating applied to the entire surface of the aircraft.
  • ----------
  • From the OVA "GUNDAM EVOLVE../9", MSZ-006-3B Zeta Gundam Unit 3 Type B Gray Zeta is made into a three-dimensional Gunpla for the first time!
  • The body shape and special weapons are reproduced with new modeling, and it also supports the transformation gimmick into Wave Rider!
  • ● Reproduce many characteristic body armor shapes!
  • Shoulder armor, front armor, side armor, rear armor, forearm armor, leg side armor, tail stabilizer,
  • The stabilizer is reproduced with new modeling.
  • ● Includes Gray Zeta's special weapon!
  • Gray Zeta's special weapons, Beam Cannon and Mega Gatling, are also included with new modeling.
  • Beam Cannon: A large weapon suitable for an aircraft with enhanced firepower.
  • Mega Gatling: A shield-integrated weapon with a built-in beam machine gun.
  • ● Transforms into Wave Rider!
  • It is possible to transform into the atmospheric entry mode/Waverider, which is a feature of Zeta Gundam.
  • The large beam cannon, newly shaped tail stabilizer, etc. give it a significantly different impression from the regular Wave Rider.

  • Coloring stickers (Tetron stickers) to reproduce the characteristic camouflage pattern and Gray Wolf's personal mark.
  • A slide mark (water transfer decal) containing caution marks is included.
