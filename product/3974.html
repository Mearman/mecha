  • In-store sales item Introducing an expansion parts set that can be combined with the MS body (sold separately) to reproduce various forms of Gundam Barbatos!
  • ---
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam Iron-Blooded Orphans", you can reproduce each armed form equipped by "Gundam Barbatos".
  • Weapons and optional parts are commercialized as an expansion set.
  • The first 1/100 scale mobile worker is newly included!
  • *The MS body is not included with this product.
  • *To reproduce each form, "MG 1/100 Gundam Barbatos" (separately sold in-store item) is required before assembly.
  • ● Weapons and optional parts that can reproduce the 1st, 2nd, 3rd, 5th, 5th ground battle specifications and 6 forms are reproduced with new modeling.
  • Equipped with details and gimmicks that contain the density and amount of information unique to MG.
  • 1st form
  • The armor on both shoulders has been removed.
  • 2nd form
  • Newly sculpted armor on both shoulders.
  • 3rd form
  • The characteristic wire claw on the left arm is equipped with an unfolding gimmick.
  • The injection scene can be reproduced using the included lead wire.
  • 5th form
  • Reproduce each equipment for the Earth descent operation with new modeling.
  • The lumbar booster, which was adapted from Schwalbe Glaze's, has a thruster section that can be deployed.
  • The arm mortar reproduces the movable gimmick when shooting.
  • Fifth form ground battle specification
  • A new reproduction of a wrench mace that is as long as the MS itself.
  • The arm machine gun and enlarged legs are thoroughly reproduced with new parts.
  • 6th form
  • Based on the fifth form, the shape and coloring of the armor on the upper body are three-dimensionalized with new parts.
  • Faithfully reproduces the lumbar booster for the 6th form.
  • ● Mobile worker included!
  • The first three-dimensional version of a 1/100 scale mobile worker.
  • In addition to being able to reproduce general aircraft and commander aircraft with a selection formula, it is equipped with a gimmick that opens and closes the cockpit.
  • ● A newly sculpted Olga Itsuka figure (standing) is included.
  • ● Newly designed water transfer decal included!
  • In addition to the Tekkadan logo, it includes a variety of markings such as Gjallarhorn, the Baudouin family crest, and decals to express damage.
  • <Set contents>
  • Left arm parts for 1st and 2nd forms / Shoulder armor for 2nd and 3rd forms / Wire claw for 3rd form / Wrench mace /
  • Chest reactive armor / arm mortar / lumbar arm / 5th form lumbar booster / 6th form armor / arm machine gun /
  • Waist booster for 6th form / Mobile worker / Olga Itsuka figure (standing) / Water transfer decal
