  • From the new plastic model series "30 MINUTES FANTASY" that allows you to create your own fantasy job, "Rosa Knight" is now available!
  • ■A starter set that includes armor and armed parts in addition to the base "silhouette" [elementary body].

  • ■By providing movable axes on the head and neck, it is possible to pull the chin, stick out the face, etc.
  • ■The newly designed shoulder joint allows you to maintain your body's line even in bold poses.
  • ■The movable axes of the chest, torso, and waist work together with the sliding mechanism at the base of the waist joint to create natural poses.
  • ■Comes with armor and armed parts to “job change” to “Rosa Knight” by combining with “Silhouette” [base body].
  • ■A new mechanism for attaching shoulder and waist armor via joints on the back allows for flexible movement.
  • ■Swords and shields can be mounted on the back or waist using joint parts.
  • ■The element core's attribute is "fire".
  • ■The face design and coloring are different from Liber Republic's "Silhouette" and "Night" which are sold separately.
