  • The dummy balloon that appeared in "Mobile Suit Gundam: Char's Counterattack" is now available as a painted soft vinyl that does not require assembly and can be enjoyed side by side with the 1/144 scale!

  • The model is based on the one carried by Re-Gazi in the movie, and the shape of the chest and backpack are also modeled after Re-Gazi.
  • In addition, the product tag has a design inspired by the old kit package at the time of the broadcast.
