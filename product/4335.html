Details

  • Gundam Mk-III, joining the HG series!
  • From "Mobile Suit Z Gundam" MSV, Gundam Mk-III, developed as AEUGO's next-generation MS prototype, is commercialized in the HG series.
  • Each characteristic body part is reproduced with new modeling, and the impressive body color is expressed with molding color.
  • ● Thoroughly reproduces the Gundam Mk-III's body shape with new modeling
  • The body shape that makes up Gundam Mk-III, including the distinctive head, chest, shoulders, legs, and backpack, is reproduced with new modeling parts.
  • head
  • A new reproduction of the head with a distinctive sharp shape.
  • Chest, shoulders, feet
  • The characteristic chest, torso, shoulders, and leg armor are reproduced.
  • Details such as the shape around the cockpit and exhaust ducts are reproduced in detail, and the red exterior on the sides of the shoulders and legs are color-coded for each part.
  • back pack
  • Reproduces a backpack equipped with dual beam cannons.
  • The cannon can be adjusted up and down.
  • ● Comes with a wealth of armaments such as a beam rifle and special shield!
  • The impressive special shield is reproduced with new modeling.
  • Additionally, two types of beam rifles and large beam saber effects are included.
  • Beam saber (beam cannon)
  • By removing the barrel of the double beam cannon, you can recreate a large beam saber!
  • Dedicated grip parts are included for left and right.
  • Dedicated shield
  • A new reproduction of the characteristic shield equipped by Gundam Mk-III.
  • The beam saber attached to the shield is removable.
  • Attached weapons: Beam rifle (2 types) / Beam saber (Beam cannon) / Shield / Beam saber
