Details

  • This is a PG series plastic model of Strike Rouge that appears in "Mobile Suit Gundam SEED DESTINY".
  • A new Destiny version costume, Cagalli, is included in the MS as a pilot figure.
  • The five movable fingers on both hands, which were previously five separate fingers, are now integrated into one, making assembly easier.
  • Like past PGs, this model has an internal movable skeleton, and the head camera eye also lights up with LEDs.
  • Equipment includes a rifle, shield, and assault knife.
  • Sky Grasper has been arranged in a molding color that is more conscious of weight.
  • Comes with a stand that allows you to recreate poses from the movie.
