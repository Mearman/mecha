Details

  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam UC (Unicorn)" comes the Jesta Cannon, a heavily armed MS equipped with a large firearm!
  • The Jesta Cannon, a heavily armed type with enhanced fire control ability that appears in "Mobile Suit Gundam UC episode 6" and "Mobile Suit Gundam UC episode 7", has been commercialized as a 1/100 scale Master Grade series.
  • Each part that has been given distinctive armament and increased armor due to the heavy armoring has been reproduced with new modeling!
  • ● Thorough reproduction of additional weapons such as beam cannon!
  • The beam cannon for medium-range support and the 4-unit multi-launcher can be adjusted in angle, allowing you to recreate dynamic scenes.
  • The beam rifle comes with newly designed expansion equipment that can be attached and removed, such as a grenade launcher.
  • The triple grenade launcher added along with the increased leg armor is equipped with an opening/closing gimmick.
  • Each additional weapon can be attached and detached.
  • ● Reproduce the characteristic body shape such as increased armor with new modeling!
  • The additional armor on the chest, shoulders, waist front armor, left arm, and leg are reproduced with new modeling.
  • ● Water transfer decal included!
  • Newly designed markings including caution marks are included as water transfer decals.
  • ● By replacing parts, it is possible to remove some additional armor and attach a shield.
  • Attached weapons: Beam cannon / 4-barreled multi launcher / Beam rifle / Beam saber /
  • Vulcan Pod System / Hand Grenade / Shield
