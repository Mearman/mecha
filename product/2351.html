Details

  • ■The long-awaited “Scope Dog” from “Armored Trooper Votoms” is now available as a 1/20 scale kit.
  • ■The fuselage, which is the cockpit, uses a new method called ``bottle-on snap'' that uses extremely small bolt-shaped parts to fix the armor plate to the frame.
  • ■The pilot figure's helmet is made into separate parts, including the visor, which is made into a clear part.
  • ■The distinctive head scope combines transparent parts and jewel stickers to thoroughly reproduce details such as the depth of the camera lens.
  • ■The legs reproduce the movable mechanism of the turn pick and the gliding wheel on the sole of the foot.
  • ■The heavy machine gun reproduces the structure reminiscent of a real gun, such as the barrel, magazine, and stock, and uses an assembly method.
