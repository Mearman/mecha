  • --- Double O equipped with seven swords ---
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam 00V", 00 Gundam Seven Sword, which carries seven swords,
  • Commercialized in the RG series, which pursues unparalleled detailed expression and "realism" in 1/144.
  • In addition to the GN beam saber, which is shared equipment with 00 Gundam,
  • Armed with GN Buster Sword II / GN Sword II Long / GN Sword II Short / GN Qatar etc.
  • Thoroughly reproduced with new RG-compliant modeling!
  • SEVEN SWORD Seven Sword －－－－－－
  • ● GN BUSTER SWORDII [GN Buster Sword II]
  • Equipped with a blade slide mechanism and a grip slide mechanism, it can transform into both GN shield mode and GN sword mode.
  • Can be equipped on the GN drive part and arm part.
  • The maximum total length is approximately 163mm.
  • A pose-holding stand is included that allows you to stably display the large GN Buster Sword II.
  • ● GN SWORD II LONG [GN Sword II Long]
  • When carrying it, mount it on your right hip.
  • ● GN SWORD II SHORT [GN SWORD II SHORT]
  • When carried, the short that is mounted on the left hip reproduces the injection form of the tip with a lead wire.
  • ● GN KATAR [GN Qatar]
  • The blade wrapped in GN particles is reproduced with clear parts.
  • The left and right knee armor equipped with two GN Qatars is also reproduced.
  • ● GN BEAM SABER [GN Beam Saber]
  • Carry two on your waist.
  • ★ Newly designed realistic decals included!
