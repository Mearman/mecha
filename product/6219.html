  • From the "Super Robot Wars OG" series, the long-awaited "Gunleon", which can be replaced to reproduce Magna mode, is now available!
  • ■Magna mode can be reproduced by deforming each part and replacing parts.
  • ■It comes with a variety of weapons, including the 230mm long Riot Jalenti, which is longer than the entire length of the aircraft.
  • ■``Riot Jalenti'' partially unfolds and can be transformed into Magna mode.
  • ■The "Giegu Gun" can be stored in the adjustable boom on the back, and the "Decker Spanner" can be stored in the legs.
  • ■Jewel stickers are used on the red part of the waist, and plastic stickers are used on the black and orange parts of the sides of the "Chain Decutter".
  • ■Action base 7 is included to expand the range of poses.
