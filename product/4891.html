  • Based on the kit bundled with the game software ``Mobile Suit Gundam Side Stories'', a number of new land battle equipment are included!
  • Adopts a sharp molding color that matches the image of ground combat using heavy ground combat equipment!
  • ● Numerous weapons that reproduce the heavy equipment specifications for land battles!
  • In addition to the beam saber that reproduces the dual-wielding pose, there is also a 180mm cannon, 90mm bullpup machine gun, leg missile pod, etc.
  • Ground battle equipment is included with new modeling.
  • The large 180mm cannon can be mounted on the waist and unfolded to recreate a shooting pose, and when carried it can be folded and mounted on the back.
  • A spike shield with a telescopic gimmick on the pile driver at the tip is also included.
  • ● You can reproduce the appearance when HADES is activated!
  • Comes with two colored visors, clear green molded and clear red molded,
  • Normal state and HADES activation state can be reproduced by selecting the visor and seal.
