Details

  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam" MSV (Mobile Suit Variation), Zogok is now available in Jaburo strategy format!
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam" MSV, the amphibious MS Zogok is commercialized in the HGUC series.
  • ● Reproduce the setting color of the Jaburo Strategy Specification, which has a light blue and green color scheme!
  • ● Thoroughly reproduces the shape of characteristic weapons such as rod arms and boomerang cutters.
  • The rod arm can be expanded and contracted by replacing parts, and the arm punch can be reproduced!
  • The 10 boomerang cutters are removable.
  • ● A ball joint is used at the base of the mono-eye, allowing for expressive changes in line of sight.
  • ● Sturm Faust can be mounted on the back waist.
  • ● Two types of heat saber blades are included: clear and normal molding (selectable).
  • ● In addition to gripping hands (left and right), weapon handles (left and right) are included.
  • ● MSV-specific markings are included as new stickers!
  • Attached weapons: Arm punch, boomerang cutter, Sturm Faust, heat saber
