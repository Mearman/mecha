  • A type and L type are now available in master grade with new modeling!
  • <Two types of mission packs can be reproduced!
  • ● A (assault) type with long-distance invasion specifications
  • The long-range invasion pack, which bears the name "ASSAULT" and is equipped with additional mobile units and a beam bazooka, is reproduced with new modeling.
  • Increased mobile unit
  • A new reproduction of the increased mobility unit installed on the backpack and shoulder hard points!
  • The shoulder hard point joints can be expanded and retracted, allowing for a range of motion in the arms depending on the pose.
  • The thruster part is movable with a ball joint.
  • propellant tank
  • Propellant tanks can be equipped on the forearms and legs.
  • beam bazooka
  • A new beam bazooka is included.
  • The energy pack is removable and can be attached to the hard point on the side of the waist.
  • ● L (long range) type with long range specifications
  • The long-range pack, which bears the name "LONG-RANGE", is equipped with a long rifle, backpack, and various sensors, and is reproduced with a completely new model.
  • long rifle
  • Precisely reproduces the long rifle, which is the biggest feature of the L type.
  • It is suspended by the waist stay at the lumbar hard point, allowing it to be deployed forward and stowed to the rear.
  • Reproduces the deployment of the foregrip, the folding of the gun barrel, the attachment and detachment of the magazine and energy pack.
  • The body bullet spare magazine can be installed on the side of the waist, and the energy pack can be installed on the leg hard points.
  • The rear fin unit is movable.
  • shoulder unit
  • Beam saber rack on right shoulder hard point,
  • Equipped with a compound sensor for precision aiming on the left shoulder hard point.
  • The sensor fin unit is movable.
  • backpack
  • The thermoscope equipped in the backpack is deployed.
  • The thruster part is movable with a ball joint.
  • twin missile launcher
  • Equipped with dual missile launchers on both forearms.
  • ● Water transfer decal included.
  • Includes water transfer decals including original markings.
