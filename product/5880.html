Details

  • The heavy tank “Ankylosaurus” from the Cretaceous period appears in the 6th edition of “Planosaurus”, a brand that lets you “know everything about dinosaurs” with your own hands!
  • ■You can learn about the internal structure of dinosaurs with "Skeleton Build".
  • ■ "Dinosaur Build" allows you to restore the dinosaur's appearance using specifications unique to plastic models, where the skin parts are attached to the top of the skeleton.
  • ■Easy to assemble as no tools such as nippers or adhesives are required.
  • ■The characteristic armor that covers the entire body reproduces the arrangement pattern based on the closely related armored dragon.
  • ■The hammer-shaped protrusion at the tip of the tail, which is thought to have been able to crush the shin bone of an attacking Tyrannosaurus, is also faithfully reproduced.
  • ■The bony tendon that connects to the bony mass at the tip of the tail is also expressed.
  • ■Faithfully reproduces the robust skull with no characteristic gaps of Ankylosaurus.
  • ■Various parts such as opening and closing the mouth, front legs, hind legs, and neck can be moved, allowing you to display it in your favorite pose.
