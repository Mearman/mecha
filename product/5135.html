  • From the side story "Kyoukai Senki Frost Flower" serialized in Monthly Hobby Japan, we have commercialized the aircraft Setsuro, which is used in the "Aurora no Kyokuhin" along with Meires Byakuchi.
  • The blue-based aircraft color is reproduced with molding color.
  • ● Reproduce the “Setsuro” aircraft coloring
  • The old model Jawhound used by the North American Alliance was captured and the tuned-up Setsuro is reproduced in molding colors based on blue and gray.
  • ● Various weapons and equipment are included in new shapes.
  • A winch unit and spare grenade bullet case are included with new modeling.
  • winch unit
  • The winch unit attached to the shoulder reproduces the injection state with lead wires.
  • leg thread
  • In addition to the normal state of the legs, parts can be selected to reproduce the state in which the "sled" equipment for moving in wetlands is attached.
  • ● Parked aircraft condition can be reproduced
  • The joint mechanism in the legs allows you to recreate the parking situation while standing on your knees.
