  • ● Tallgeese IIEW (equipped with heat saber)
  • The impressive heat saber and special handle that Tallgeese IIEW was equipped with in the movie are reproduced with new modeling.
  • heat saber
  • A three-dimensional version of a large melee weapon that exceeds the total length of Tallgeese IIEW.
  • The color of the saber is reproduced by molding color for each part, and the pattern of the tsuba is finely reproduced by molding.
  • sheath
  • The sheath that can store the heat saber is reproduced with new parts.
  • ● Altron Gundam EW (Futier equipment)
  • The Futier (butterfly) of Altron Gundam EW that appeared in the movie is reproduced with a new model.
  • Foutier (Phalaenopsis)
  • The beam cannon and wings equipped on the backpack are three-dimensional.
  • The arm part of the beam cannon is flexible and the wing part is equipped with an expansion gimmick.
  • Furthermore, a beam trident can be equipped on the side of the wing.
  • Parts that can reproduce the coloring in the play are included!
  • It is possible to reproduce the coloring in the play where some of the armor on the shoulders and arms is yellow.
  • ● Gundam Deathscythe Hell EW (equipped with Hornet)
  • It is possible to reproduce the hornet equipped with twin beam scissors and side armor.
  • twin beam scissors
  • Includes new parts to expand the tip of the beam size.
  • A set of 1 green beam effect.
  • hornet
  • Two buster shields added to the sides of the waist are included.
  • The coloring in the play is reproduced with molded colors, and the claw part is also equipped with an unfolding gimmick.
  • ● New water slide decals designed by Hajime Katoki for expansion parts are included!
