Details

  • The Federation battleship "Albion" that appeared in the OVA "Mobile Suit Gundam 0083 Stardust Memory" is now available as a 1/1700 scale model.
  • Each part of the hull is coated with matte plating in the set color.
  • By replacing parts, the deployment and storage states of the mega particle cannon and catapult can be reproduced.
  • The left and right catapults are reproduced with metal parts.
  • "Gundam GP01" and "Gundam GP02A" of the same scale are included.
