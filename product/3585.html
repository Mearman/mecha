  • A fierce flame god carrying Heat Lancer.
  • From ``Mobile Suit Gundam Gaiden Missing Link'', we have commercialized the aircraft flown by Captain Doug Schneid, who leads the special competitive unit ``Marcosias Squad''.
  • Parts such as the heat lancer, which is a characteristic equipment, and parts such as the forearm are reproduced with new modeling, and the styling that matches the setting is faithfully reproduced.
  • ● Reproduce Heat Lancer and part of the main body with new molded parts
  • The special weapons "Heat Lancer" and "Triple 35mm Gatling Cannon" are reproduced with new modeling.
  • In addition, new parts have been adopted that reproduce the details and color coding of the forearms, etc.
  • ● Reproduce your personal color with molded color
  • The personal color based on purple, which was inherited by the later Ifrit Schneid, is reproduced in molding color.
  • In addition, an emblem sticker of the Marcosias squad is included.
  • ● Reproduce the image from the play with distinctive weapons
  • In addition to the Heat Lancer and triple 35mm Gatling cannon, a "shotgun" is also included.
  • With a wide range of motion, it is possible to reproduce the battle image in the play.
  • Attached weapons: Heat Lancer / Triple 35mm Gatling gun / Shotgun
