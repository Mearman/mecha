Details

  • Three aircraft from "Star Wars: The Last Jedi", "Millennium Falcon", "Boosted X-Wing Fighter Poe's Special Machine", and "X-Wing Fighter Resistance Blue Squadron Specification", have been made into a new vehicle set.
  • The Millennium Falcon has details such as the antenna and each part of the aircraft that match the new Star Wars: The Last Jedi version.
  • Boosted X-Wing Fighter Poe's special machine also reproduces the "Boosted Accelerator Pod" engine part of "Star Wars: The Last Jedi" version!
  • The X-Wing Fighter Resistance Blue Squadron version comes with a droid that has been changed to the "Star Wars: The Last Jedi" version, and the line of the aircraft has also been changed.
  • Overall length: Millennium Falcon: Approx. 99mm/X-Wing Fighter Poe exclusive machine: Approx. 87mm/X-Wing Fighter (Resistance specification): Approx. 87mm
