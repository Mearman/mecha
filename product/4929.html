  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam Iron-Blooded Orphans", Schwalbe Glaze, operated by Gaelio Baudouin, is commercialized as an HG series kit.
  • The body color of Gaelio's machine is reproduced with molded color, and the right arm is equipped with a special lance!
  • ● Reproduce the aircraft color that appears in the play with molded color!
  • ● Comes with a special lance!
  • A special lance consisting of a rifle (short barrel) and a lance unit is included in white molding color.
  • ● Comes with a variety of weapons that expand the range of your display!
  • In addition to a number of weapons including a special lance, a shield for Glaze is also included.
  • The wire claw can reproduce the injection state using the included lead wire.
  • ● Centering on the bold movable structure of the upper body, the twisting movement creates a sense of dynamism.
  • Achieves high installation ease due to the wide range of motion of the leg structure.
  • The internal structure of the head, which incorporates spherical parts, is also reproduced.
  • Attached weapons: Lance for Schwalbe Glaze / Rifle / Battle Ax / Wire Claw / Shield
