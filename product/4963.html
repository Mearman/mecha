  • --- "RG Sinanju" (sold separately) linked items are now available!

  • RG 1/144 5 types of items and parts to enjoy Sinanju even more are now available as an expansion set!
  • Many impressive scenes can be reproduced!
  • 〈Set contents〉
  • ● Rocket bazooka × 2
  • ● Beam ax effect x 2
  • ● Damaged head
  • ● Lt. Col. Dagda figure
  • ● Hand parts (grip hand) × 1 each for left and right
  • ◆◆ Rocket Bazooka
  • The rocket bazooka, a large solid weapon exclusively for Sinanju, is reproduced with a new model with RG-compliant details!
  • Equipped with a telescopic and merging gimmick, it can be joined with beam rifles and shields.
  • Two impressive bazookas are included.
  • ・The rocket bazooka reproduces the setting from the movie, which is useful when handling it while carrying it and mounting it with other weapons.
  • Equipped with a gun barrel extension gimmick.
  • - On the back of the shield, a rocket bazooka and beam ax can be carried and equipped at the same time.
  • Comes with a special rocket/bazooka joint that allows for shooting poses while attached to the shield.
  • ◆◆ Effects for beam ax
  • Two effects with different shapes from those included in the product version are included.
  • An impressive scene in the TV opening video, etc., where the beam ax is connected and rotated in a beam naginata state.
  • Reproduce the beam radiation of the imagined sword muscle.
  • - A moving shape that allows you to feel the nuances of rotation as a beam naginata.
  • In addition to joining the two pieces of the same type that come with this product to the axe, you can also use the effects that come with the product version with different shapes.
  • A combined display is also possible.
  • ◆◆ Damaged head
  • Reproduces the damaged head that is impressive in the scene in the movie.
  • It comes with a compatible specification that does not require recombination with normal head parts, and the head can be replaced to suit the scene.
  • ◆◆ Figure
  • The same scale figure that reproduces Lieutenant Colonel Dagda saluting.
  • ◆◆ Hand parts
  • Hand grip parts (left and right) that expand the range of poses, such as recreating the starting scene in the middle of the story.
  • ◆ Comes with realistic decals for weapons, etc.
  • *The beam rifle used to mount the rocket bazooka is included with the MS product.
  • *RG Sinanju used in dioramas etc. is painted.
  • *The figures used in the diorama are painted.
  • *The back of the hand is from the main body MS (sold separately).
