  • ■Reproduces the characteristic two rows of teeth in the front and back of the upper jaw.
  • ■The Mosasaurus, a marine reptile, is said to have been covered with fine scales similar to those of iguanas and monitor lizards, giving it a detailed and delicate skin structure.
  • ■A display pedestal is included that can be displayed while swimming.
  • ■ Various parts such as opening and closing of the mouth, fins, neck and tail can be moved, allowing you to recreate the figure swimming.
