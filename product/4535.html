  • The W type, a mission pack developed for the prototype MS Gundam F90 of the "Formula Plan" by Sanary (S・N・R・I), is now available in master grade with a completely new model!
  • The newly drawn W type can be transformed into a flight form.
  • ● W (Warbird) military aircraft specifications
  • A new mission pack created by Kunio Okawara in the F90 A to Z PROJECT.
  • The mission pack, which bears the name "WARBIRD" and gives the F90 a variable mechanism for flight mode when installed, is reproduced with a new model.
  • armor
  • Reproduces the armor attached to the shoulders and waist.
  • The front armor that covers the front of the waist reproduces color coding by dividing the parts.
  • wing
  • Reproduce the wing deployment mechanism installed on the back.
  • Landing gear (arm)
  • The claw-shaped landing gear attached to the arm is equipped with a movable gimmick.
  • ● W (Warbird) flight form
  • The backpack moves above the head and each part expands, allowing it to transform into a flying form.
  • Beam effect parts can be attached to the wing on the back, which is a Minovsky craft.
  • beam cannon
  • The nose part expands and a beam cannon appears.
  • Landing gear (waist)
  • In addition to the arms, the front armor parts can be expanded to form the landing gear.
  • Wing beam effect
  • Beam effect parts are reproduced in clear pink.
  • ● Water transfer decal included.
  • Comes with water transfer decals including original markings.
