  • RX124 TR-6 [Woundwort]

  • Gundam TR-6 [Woundwort] is the first Gunpla in the HGUC series!
  • New modeling reproduces unique styling and transformation to MA mode!
  • ● Thoroughly reproduce Gundam TR-6 (Woundwort) with new modeling!
  • ・Becomes the core MS of the weapon "TR-6" developed as the culmination of the TR series,
  • The body shape of "Woundwort" is reproduced with new modeling.
  • -Thoroughly reproduces the design and details that combine individuality and a unique atmosphere.
  • The head with a complex surface configuration is faithfully reproduced according to the setting.
  • ● Reproduce the transformation into mobile armor!
  • - The transformation into mobile armor, which is a characteristic of the aircraft, is reproduced by replacing parts.
  • - MA form is reproduced by folding the legs and replacing the special head part with the booster pod as the core.
  • The wing that is deployed in MA form can be reproduced by attaching special parts.
  • ● Composite shield booster can be transformed into a rifle or claw!
  • ・Multi-functional shield 'Composite Shield Booster' has a rifle mode for shooting,
  • Changing to claw mode can also be reproduced by replacing parts.
  • -The total length of the rifle is approximately 225mm.
  • The tip of the bellows arm can be connected to the armor on the shoulder.
  • - By separating the bellows arm and shield part and attaching a lead wire, it is possible to create a claw ejection state.
  • The beam port on the back of the claw is also reproduced by replacing parts.
  • ● A wide variety of poses are possible with the wide movable structure and deformation mechanism of the whole body!
  • Attached weapons: Composite shield booster
