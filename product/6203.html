Details

  • A master grade is now available with the motif of the colors and helmet of the "Nanban Dogusoku" (displayed in the treasure room of Nikko Toshogu Shrine), which is said to have been worn by Ieyasu Tokugawa during the Battle of Sekigahara!
  • ■ The main body molding color is based on black, reproducing the image of "Nanban Dogusoku", and the main body and weapons are plated with black and chrome.
  • ■The red of Kusazuri is expressed with a sticker made of washi paper, and the family crest of trefoiled Aoi is expressed with a glossy sticker.
  • ■A wealth of weapons included that allows you to enjoy various action pose displays as a Gunpla.
  • Commemorating the 25th anniversary of “Shrines and Temples in Nikko” being registered as a World Heritage Site, “Gunpla” is dedicated to Nikko Toshogu Shrine!
  • "MG 1/100 Musha Gundam Mk-II Tokugawa Ieyasu Nanban Dogusoku Ver." will be dedicated on May 26, 2024, and pre-sale will begin at Nikko Toshogu Shrine from the 27th.
  • *Sales are planned at a later date at the official Gunpla facility "THE GUNDAM BASE", Bandai Namco Group's official mail order site "Premium Bandai", and other events.
