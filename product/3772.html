Details

  • "Gundam Kyrios" from "Mobile Suit Gundam 00" has been made into an MG based on the latest drawings by Noriyuki Yanase!
  • ■In accordance with the original setting, it uses a frame that shares some parts with the MG 1/100 Gundam Dynames.
  • ■Transformation to MA form is reproduced by frame structure!
  • ■By locking the shoulder armor and the nose of the backpack when in MA mode, form retention is strengthened and a stable display is possible.
  • ■Hand parts reproduce the same deformation mechanism as the anime.
  • ■The GN beam submachine gun connected to the arm joint is replaceable.
  • ■The wireless grip can be attached to two weapons as configured.
  • ■GN shield can be deployed.
  • ■GN hand missile unit can also be equipped on the arm.
