  • Figure-riseLABO [Figure-rise LABO]
  • A project that researches expressions that could not be achieved with conventional figures and plastic models, and evolves toward realization.
  • The [Special Coating] version of the 4th series ``Figure-riseLABO Shikinami Asuka Langley'' is now available.
  • A special limited edition model with a metallic coating applied to part of the "test plug suit" to give it a beautiful shine.
  • ● Shine due to coating process [Special coating]
  • A red metallic coating is applied to the arms and legs of the test plug suit.
  • The texture of the suit and Asuka's supple body line are expressed with a glossy glow.
  • ● Expressing the transparent texture and close fit of a suit [Overlaid injection]
  • Using new technology, the body part is molded with two-colored inserts of clear orange and skin color, expressing the sheerness and tightness of the test plug suit.
  • Delicate mold processing changes the thickness of the suit and expresses the shadows of the suit.
  • ● Reproduces line-of-sight tracking [Eye tracking injection]
  • By devising the iris part of the eyes, we were able to follow Asuka's line of sight as if she were looking at us.
