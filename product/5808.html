Details

  • From the movie ``Mobile Suit Gundam: The Island of Cucrus Doan'', the RGM-79 GM (Moroccan front specification) that appears in the town of Casablanca has been commercialized by HG.
  • The characteristic aircraft shape and coloring are reproduced with molded colors.
  • In addition to the aircraft identification number mark depicted in the movie, a new water transfer decal including an original design caution mark is included.
  • -Reproduce the gym of the Moroccan front with new modeling
  • The gym that fights the Southern Cross squad in the town of Casablanca is recreated with new modeling.
  • The head, chest, and shoulder shapes are faithfully three-dimensional, which is different from Jim (Slegger's aircraft).
  • The coloring, mainly orange and light gray, is faithfully reproduced by color-coding by dividing the parts.
  • Head/Thorax Cockpit Hatch/Shoulders
  • The shape of each part, such as the side duct and cockpit hatch, which have a different shape from the Slegger aircraft, is faithfully reproduced down to the smallest details.
  • The main camera uses green clear parts.
  • - Comes with weapons such as shoulder cannon and beam spray gun (early model).
  • In addition to the back shoulder cannon, beam rifle, beam saber, and shield, a beam spray gun (early model) is included.
  • Hand parts such as weapon holders and open hands are also included to expand the range of poses.
  • -New water transfer decal included.
  • A water slide decal is included that includes a number mark indicating that it is a platoon aircraft that appeared in the play.
  • Attached weapons: Beam spray gun (early model) / Beam rifle / Shield / Beam saber / Shoulder cannon
