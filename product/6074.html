  • The Hull Fighter, a mission pack and fighter aircraft bearing the name "NEXT", is reproduced with a new model.
  • hull fighter
  • The Hull Fighter, which is a mission pack and fighter aircraft, can be transformed and combined with the Gundam F90.
  • The Hull Fighter in fighter plane status can also be recreated in flight using the display base.
  • The canopy can be opened and closed.
  • Beam Rifle/Hilt Funnel
  • The beam rifle, which is the exclusive armament for the N type, can be equipped with a total of 6 hilt funnels.
  • The removed hilt funnel can be recreated in funnel mode and beam saber mode by replacing parts.
  • shield
  • The shield, which is part of the nose of the Hull Fighter, can be equipped on the arm.
  • Hilt Funnel can also be attached to the side of the shield.
  • Equipped with a beam rifle to reproduce the shooting form.

  • The Hull Fighter is equipped with hard points and appeared in the manga "Mobile Suit Gundam F90FF".
  • Mission packs sold separately can be equipped with N types such as NB type and NG type.
  • It is also possible to remove the main wing part and replace it with a V-type Vesbar unit (sold separately).

  • F90 No. 2 with roll-out color is included!
