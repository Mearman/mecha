Details

  • A compatible PG plastic model of the main character Gundam GP01/FB that appears in the OVA "Mobile Suit Gundam 0083".
  • Completely reproduces the replacement gimmicks for ground battles and space battles, as well as the complete transformation and combination gimmicks of the core fighter.
  • A large maintenance hanger is also included, allowing you to attach and display replacement parts for each part.
  • The head camera eye is illuminated by LED, and the maintenance hatches on each part can be opened and closed.
  • Weapons include a beam rifle, machine gun, beam saber, and shield.
