  • The Gunigi land battle type belonging to the "Bluebird Squad" that appeared in "Mobile Suit V Gundam New Mobile Suit Variation" is now three-dimensional with RE/100!

  • ● Faithfully reproduces the aircraft color of the setting drawing!
  • Based on the RE/100 Gunigi land battle type, the bright blue color that gave rise to the name "Bluebird Corps" is reproduced in the molding color.
  • In addition, stickers are used for the shoulder ducts, knees, and various cameras, and the details are color-coded.
  • ● Newly designed water transfer decal included!
  • The distinctive aircraft markings of the Bluebird Corps specification aircraft are reproduced with newly designed water transfer decals.
  • ● Various weapons of Gunigi are included!
  • A wide variety of weapons are included, allowing you to recreate a variety of poses.
