  • head

  • A yellow beam effect can be attached to the mega particle cannon.
  • wrist

  • The effect of the beam claw part is reproduced with green clear parts.
  • body
  • The shape is thoroughly reproduced, from the chest armor to the vernier feather funnel on the back.
  • Furthermore, it has a built-in deformation gimmick that works with the arms and legs.
  • feet
  • Beam effects can be attached to the sharp-looking ankles.
  • Like the arm, flexible movement is achieved through indirect movement.
  • beam ax
  • The beam ax with its distinctive beam generator is also newly modeled.
  • A large band-shaped effect can be attached.

  • Reproduce the transformation to MA form like "hand" by replacing parts.
