Details

  • Crossbone Gundam X2, the black space pirate controlled by Sabine, is now available in the HGUC series!
  • From "Mobile Suit Crossbone Gundam", Crossbone Gundam X2 is commercialized in the HGUC series.
  • Reproduce the distinctive body shape and weapons of Crossbone Gundam X2!
  • ● A new shot lancer is included!
  • A new model of the Shot Lancer, one of the most iconic weapons used by Crossbone Gundam X2, is included.
  • With Gunpla original details, the expression is richly reproduced.
  • ● Reproduce the head shape!
  • The head has a partially different shape from the X1, and the blade antenna has a shape that changes the angle at the top.
  • The side design is also reproduced with a mold.
  • ● Comes with a variety of weapons and hand parts!
  • In addition to the shot lancer, it comes with a variety of weapons such as an ABC cloak, scissor anchor, and more.
  • There are also plenty of weapon-specific hand parts that allow you to stably hold your unique weapon.
  • Attached weapons: Shot Lancer / Scissor Anchor / Heat Dagger / Beam Saber /
  • Zanbuster (Beam Zanbar/Buster Gun) / Brand Marker / Beam Shield
