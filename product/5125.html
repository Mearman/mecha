  • From the official side story "Mobile Suit Gundam SEED ECLIPSE", the Gin Glidiator that stands in front of the Eclipse Gundam has been made into a 1/100 scale MG series figure.
  • The heavily armored and heavily armed aircraft design is reproduced with new modeling parts.
  • ● Reproduce special weapons and increased armor with new parts
  • Equipment designed by Junichi Akutsu, such as the unsheathed sword "Lichtschwerth" and the electromagnetic linear cannon "M67 Seamir", is newly sculpted.
  • The head and various parts of the armor, which are reminiscent of a gladiator's helmet, are reproduced down to the coloring of detailed parts such as red and silver.
  • ● Equipped with a large dedicated backpack equipped with various gimmicks.
  • The backpack is equipped with a large thruster and the electromagnetic linear cannon "M67 Seamir" and has multiple movable mechanisms.
  • In addition to the electromagnetic linear cannon being movable via the ball joint, the thruster covers can be expanded.
  • ● Various actions are possible with weapons worthy of the name “Gladiator”
  • Unsheathed sword “Lichtschwerth”
  • A large solid sword made for destroying enemy positions can be attached to the side skirt.
  • By holding it in the left and right hand parts, various sword fights are possible.
  • M68 Pardus triple short-range guided missile launcher
  • Equipment with a hovering system added to the missile pod.
  • Reproduces the details of the missile's warhead and hovering system.
  • beaks dagger
  • Reproduces a portable weapon for use in battles against MS that can not only pierce gaps in the enemy's armor and send high voltage through it, but also twist and tear off the armor.
  • Can be mounted on the hard point of the waist.
  • ● Newly designed water transfer decal included.
  • Attached weapons: M67 Seamir electromagnetic linear cannon / unloading sword "Lichtschwerth" / Beaks dagger / M68 Pardus triple short-range guided missile launcher
