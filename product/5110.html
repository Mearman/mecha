  • The life-size ν Gundam statue "RX-93ff ν Gundam" is now available in RG-compliant definition and high-density detail!

  • ■Reproduce the long range fin funnel, which is the biggest feature of "RX-93ff ν Gundam", with new parts!
  • It is equipped with a gimmick that allows it to be deployed into a firing mode, and it can be suspended using the included new shoulder parts or attached to the arm.
  • ■Original hand parts based on the statue are included!
  • ■The shoulders and abdomen have built-in joints that allow them to move flexibly.
  • ■The shoulder armor has a built-in mechanism that expands in conjunction with the internal joints.
  • By expanding the range of motion of the shoulders, interference between parts that occurs when posing with weapons etc. is reduced.
  • ■The waist joint moves finely in each block, expanding the range of movement of the legs.
  • It is now possible to move the knees forward significantly, allowing you to create powerful poses with a wide stance even though it is 1/144 scale.
  • ■The legs are equipped with a "multi-link gimmick" that allows the armor to slide in conjunction with the movement of each joint.
