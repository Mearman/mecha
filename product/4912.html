  • --- Amphibious MS "Mass-produced Z'Gok" now available in real grade ---
  • The MSM-07 mass-produced Z'Gok is reproduced in the RG series, which pursues unparalleled detail and "realism" in 1/144!
  • From "Mobile Suit Gundam", the mass-produced Z'Gok, which appeared in numerous scenes in the play as an amphibious MS, has been commercialized in the RG series.
  • Mass-produced Z'Gok is reproduced in a 'realistic' manner with precision details unique to real grade!
  • ● Reproduced with parts division, small details, and chic molding colors that are characteristic of the RG series original expression!
  • ● In addition to realistic decals, markings for mass-produced Z'Gok are included as Tetron stickers!
  • ● By selecting parts, you can also recreate a four-claw arm!
  • ● The same scale figure of a regular soldier is included with a new model!
