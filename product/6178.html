  • Kamen Rider 45th anniversary!
  • To commemorate Kamen Rider's 45th anniversary, we have released Kamen Rider New No. 1 with a special specification that has been plated in various parts.
  • Commercialized in the "MG Figurerise Kamen Rider" series.
  • ● Each part is plated with a different color scheme and texture to recreate the scene in the play!
  • Combining two different plating textures, matte and glossy, to express every detail!
  • helmet…
  • The characteristic helmet is reproduced with light green plating.
  • Adopts exquisite color plating that allows you to recreate the settings from the movie.
  • belt…
  • Adopts glossy red plating that highlights precise details.
  • Carcass part…
  • Processed with green plating with a matte texture.
  • Wrist part…
  • Uses matte silver plating.
  • ● With the overwhelming range of motion unique to MG Figurerise, it is possible to reproduce poses from the movie!
  • The elbows and knees have double joints that allow for wide movement, allowing you to recreate dynamic poses.
  • Furthermore, the waist part has rotational movement to express natural movements similar to those of the human body.
  • ● The clear parts are molded from the back to reproduce the distinctive eyes!
  • ● With a variety of hand parts and movable fingers, you can pose impressive scenes such as transformation poses!
  • Each finger can be moved one by one, allowing for various expressions.
  • ● The muffler can also be fixed while waving in the wind to match the moving pose!
  • The muffler comes with two parts: one hanging down and one waving in the wind.
  • ● The transformation belt is reproduced in detail with color-coded precision parts and careful modeling!
  • ● Comes with a special pedestal so you can display it floating in the air!
