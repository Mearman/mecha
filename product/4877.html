Details

  • The red Gundam “Casval exclusive Gundam” is finally available in the RG (Real Grade) series!
  • The RX-78/C.A Casval exclusive Gundam, which appeared in the game software ``Mobile Suit Gundam: Gihren's Ambition'', has an impressive red image color.
  • Commercialized as a real grade series with unprecedented color-coded reproduction and flexible range of motion in 1/144 scale.
  • ● The armored parts are colored in the gradations unique to real grade, and are reproduced in red-based molding colors with different tones!
  • ● Reproduce the characteristic shape with new modeling!
  • The center part of the waist without the V design and the shield without the Federation mark are reproduced with molded parts.
  • ● The ankle guard, which is different in color from the RX-78-2 Gundam, is also reproduced in molded colors!
  • ● New water transfer decals with designs such as the Zeon military mark and aircraft model number are included!
  • Realistic decals that can create a metallic feel to the joints are also included (existing).
  • Attached weapons: Beam rifle / Hyper Bazooka / Beam saber / Shield
