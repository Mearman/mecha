Details

  • ■A set of LED units that emit UV light from the "PG 1/60 RX-0 Unicorn Gundam" (sold separately), which is molded with a special resin.
  • ■Reproduces the luminescence of Psycho Frame using resin that reacts to LED light.
  • ■The LED stored inside the fuselage is designed to match the internal clearance of the body.
  • ■Two types of LEDs are used at the tip of the head: one that lights up the camera eye and one that lights up the psycho frame.
  • ■The LEDs on the legs are structured to efficiently illuminate only the clear parts by limiting the direction in which they emit light.
  • ■A set of LED units and control units for the head, backpack, chest, waist, legs, and arms.
  • ■A convenient parts separator is included when installing the LED unit.
  • Assembly manual x 1 *Requires 4 AA batteries (sold separately).
